Pricing

Consulting fees in South Africa vary significantly based on scope, experience and engagement model. REV Improvement Consulting offers three engagement structures: Interim Executive Leadership for ongoing operational mandates, Project-Based Strategic Build for defined-scope projects with clear deliverables, and ExCo Advisory & Retainer for ongoing strategic counsel.


Fees are structured around the value delivered to your business — not hourly billing. This aligns our incentives directly with your outcomes. Contact us for a tailored proposal specific to your business challenge and budget.

Timeline

Engagement timelines depend entirely on the scope and complexity of your challenge. A focused project-based mandate — such as a market entry strategy, MVNO build, or operational turnaround plan — typically runs 6 to 12 weeks.


Interim Executive Leadership engagements are usually 3 to 12 months, aligned to the transition, vacancy or high-growth phase being navigated. Retainer-based ExCo Advisory is ongoing and reviewed quarterly. We align the timeline to your outcomes — not the other way around.

Industries

REV has deep, hands-on executive experience across four sectors: Telecommunications (MVNO builds, regulatory strategy, commercial growth), Renewable Energy (PPA development and project commercialisation), Insurance Brokerage (commercial and corporate), and Corporate Group Management (multi-entity oversight, governance and turnaround).


This cross-sector depth is a deliberate advantage — proven frameworks from one industry become competitive weapons in another. REV also works with businesses outside these four sectors where the strategic or operational challenge aligns with core competencies.

AI Strategy

AI is no longer a future consideration — it is a present competitive advantage. REV embeds practical AI tools directly into client operations: AI-assisted market analysis and revenue forecasting, process automation that reduces operational overhead, and decision-support systems that give leadership teams real-time strategic intelligence.


Every engagement considers where AI can amplify human decision-making and deliver measurable ROI — not technology for its own sake. REV's AI Adoption Roadmap service maps your specific business to practical, implementable AI use cases with clear business outcomes.

AI & Scale

South African businesses that adopt AI strategically now will outpace competitors by a significant margin over the next 3 to 5 years. AI enables scale without proportional headcount growth — automating routine processes, surfacing insights from data you already have, and enabling faster, better-informed decisions at every level of the business.


REV's AI Adoption Roadmap service maps your specific business context to practical AI use cases, builds internal capability and ensures your team drives AI-led innovation as a sustainable competitive advantage — not a one-time project. The businesses winning in South Africa tomorrow are building this capability today.
